I am broadly interested in convex optimization and machine learning algorithms. My research is focused on decision making under uncertainty using Distributionally Robust Optimization paradigm.

Since 2014, I am a doctoral student at EPFL, supervised by Prof. Daniel Kuhn and co-supervised by Prof. Peyman Mohajerin Esfahani. Before coming to EPFL, I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering from the University of Tehran.
